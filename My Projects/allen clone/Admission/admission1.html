How to Apply for APPLICATION FORM

Online Application Form:
You can fill an Online Application form through:
A. ALLEN Info & Admission App   by making payment of 500/- .
B. Our website by making payment of 500/- ( 300/- for TALLENTEX-2020 appeared students).

OMR Application Form:
You can collect Application form from Our Admission Office / Information Centers on payment of Rs.700/- only ( Rs.400/- for TALLENTEX 2020 appeared students).
(You need not to purchase an OMR Application Form if you have already filled an Online Application form)


FOR COURSE WHERE ADMISSION MODE IS DIRECT

i. You can fill Online Application Form two days before Class Commencement Date.
ii. You can also send completely filled OMR Application Form with recent Passport size color photograph to our Corporate Office (KOTA) or it can also be
submitted in person at our Admission Office OR Information Center Office.
iii. For smooth completion of Admission Process, your duly filled Application Form must reach us before the commencement of course.

FOR COURSE WHERE ADMISSION MODE IS ALLEN SCHOLARSHIP ADMISSION TEST (ASAT).

i. You can fill Online Application Form two days before ASAT Date.
ii. You can also send completely filled OMR Application Form with recent Passport size color photograph to our Corporate Office (KOTA) or it can also be submitted in person at
our Admission Office OR Information Center Office. iii. Generate & print your ASAT admit card from our website and bring the same at the opted ASAT Test Center.

NOTE:

  1. To generate and Print your ASAT Admit Card on time, your duly filled OMR Application Form must reach at least three days before the ASAT Date.
  2. For Admission in a phase, student may appear in any one of the ASAT conducted before the Course Commencement Date.
  3. You can appear in only one ASAT through One Application Form.
  4. Without Admit Card you will not be allowed to appear in ASAT at Test Center.
