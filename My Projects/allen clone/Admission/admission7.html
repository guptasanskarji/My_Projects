ALLEN Career Insitute,Kota : Kohinoor Hostel Scheme Home / Kota / Kohinoor Hostel Scheme


ALLEN provides real competitive environment exclusively for topper students by providing accommodation facilities in spacious fully furnished hostels with academic amenities and mentored by regular visits of Directors and faculties.

Initial Eligibility: NTSE 2nd Stage Qualified & should be student of our Nurture Course
1. Boarder must obey the rules and guidelines as amended by the ALLEN management from time to time
2. Boarders are not permitted to disturb their floor mates similarly they are not allowed to roam in corridors and chatting on mobile loudly.
3. Boarders have to pay the accommodation charges as framed by respective hostels before 5th of every month.
4. Selection for Kohinoor scheme is purely on academic performance and it is sole right of ALLEN to decide/define criteria for the same.
5. Scholarship criteria is fixed for the boarders although it may be amended from time to time. It is purely and strictly based on the rank secured by the boarder in their phase
CUMM. RANK SCHOLARSHIP AMOUNT*
Rank 01 Rs. 5000/-
Rank 02 to 05 Rs. 4000/-
Rank 06 to 10 Rs. 3000/-
Rank 11 to 20 Rs. 2000/-
Rank 21 to 50 Rs. 1000/-
Rank 51 to 100 Allowed to continue in hostel
Above 100 May be asked to vacate the hostel
(a).Above scheme is applicable only for students opting to stay in Kohinoor Hostels.
(b).Above formula based on 800+ and above students in a phase.
(c).Cum. Rank will be calculated on 1st of every month based on all test till date.
6. Boarders are not allowed to take their friends/ batch mates in their rooms or inside hostel premises without prior permission from the concerned wardens. They must obey and respect the warden of the concerned. They are also not permitted to visit market / any other place after 7 PM. They are however permitted to visit ALLEN if they are having extra class if any.
7. If boarders is not performing academically according to aforesaid norms as governed by the allen, he/she may be asked to vacate the hostel. He/She will be informed in advance for time duration of one month. (Security deposit will be refunded)
8. Parents are permitted to visit and stay with their wards once in a 2/3 months duration with prior permission in advance. A nominal L & B charges will be payable for it as per respective hostel rules.
9. For any query/disputes related to Kohinoor Scholarship Scheme students should contact 1.Mr. P.B.Saxena (for Medical Students) 2.Mr. P.Birla (for Engineering Students)
