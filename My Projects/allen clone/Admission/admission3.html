About ALLEN Scholarship Admission Test Home / About ALLEN Scholarship Admission Test


About ASAT : ASAT is an Offline (Pen Paper mode) test; consist of multiple choice questions as per NCERT syllabus. A student has to qualify ALLEN Scholarship Admission Test to take Admission in Yearlong ALLEN Classroom Program at Kota Center. To register for ASAT, either fill the Application Form available at ALLEN Kota Center or apply online at http://www.allen.ac.in/. ASATs are conducted on scheduled dates for Admission in the Courses starting on various dates.
