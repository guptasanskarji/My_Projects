Fee Deposition Mode Home / Fee Deposition Mode

(Application form must be submited at your nearest ALLEN Center prior to deposting fee.)

Through Net Banking / Debit Card / Credit Card / UPI (No Extra Charges)
Fee can be deposited through Net Banking / Debit Card / Credit Card / UPI facility available at Click Here

Through EMI
You can pay fee in easy EMI at no Extra Cost. For more details visit our website or Click Here


Through RTGS / NEFT
In case of ASAT, this option is available after confirm selection in ASAT / in case of Direct Admission this option is available after the Form has been scanned at ALLEN (Offline) or Form No. has been generated (Online).
Fee can be deposited by filling details in RTGS form of any bank other than HDFC Bank.
For details Click Here OR For getting details send sms RTGS to 9829988255

Through Cash Deposition in Bank
Fee in cash can also be deposited at any Branch of Central Bank of India / HDFC Bank after downloading Fee Challan from our website. (Bank Charges Extra)

Through Credit / Debit Card at office premises
Fee can also be deposited at any Center office by swiping Credit Card / Debit Card. ( No Extra Charges ).

Through CTS Cheque
Fee can also be deposited through Single Crossed CTS Cheque of the requisite amount at any of our Corporate Office / Admission Office .

  1. CTS Cheque should be in favour of "ALLEN CAREER INSTITUTE" from Nationalized Bank, payable at center office where fees is being submitted.
  2. Student must write his/her Name, Form Number, Mobile No. on the back side of CTS Cheque.
  3. Please ensure balance in the account before depositing the Fee by Cheque. cutting & over writing is not allowed. CTS Cheque should be issued from the bank account of either student himself or his/her father / mother only (Third party cheque not allowed).

BY POST: CTS Cheque can be sent through post / courier. Make sure that FEE must be received at our Corporate Office / Admission Office three days before the course commencement date.
BY HAND: To deposit CTS Cheque by hand at our office, first the student has to collect a pre-printed fee challan from our any Admission Office and fill it carefully before depositing.

Important Points to Remember

  1. Fee Deposition Time: 10:00 AM to 07:00 PM.
  2. Fee in cash is not accepted at our office. Fee in cash can be deposited only at bank branch/counter.
  3. In case of Cheque Return by any reason , Student will not be allowed in class with immediate effect & legal action will be taken under the Negotiable Instrument Act. ( CTS Cheque Return charge 500/-)

In Case of ASAT : Fee can be deposited only after qualifying the Test for courses where ASAT (ALLEN Scholarship Admission Test) is applicable.Student may deposit their fee through any one of the above modes.
